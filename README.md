# E-Commerce Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This repository showcases an advanced **E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard** built using Power BI. The dashboard transforms standard retail data into strategic actionable insights by analyzing profitability trends, discount impacts, regional performance, and product categories to drive data-backed business optimization.

## 💡 Financial KPIs & Executive Insights
The enterprise demonstrates solid market traction with **$2.30M in Total Sales**, a **12.5% Profit Margin%**, and **$286.40K in Total Profit** generated across **5,009 Total Orders**.

### 🔍 Key Business Takeaways:
* **Profitability Leakage (Discount Analysis):** The advanced *Discount vs. Profit* scatter plot clearly reveals that aggressive discounting strategies (especially below the baseline) are causing severe profit drops (red clusters), identifying a critical need for price guardrails.
* **Product Mix Strategy:** Technology leads the sales share at **36.4%**, closely followed by Furniture at **32.3%** and Office Supplies at **31.3%**, showing a highly balanced product distribution.
* **Geospatial Performance:** The West and East regions dominate the profitability chart, while the Central and South regions show lower net profit contributions, highlighting opportunities for targeted regional marketing.
* **Seasonality & Trends:** The *Monthly Sales Trend* indicates an exponential revenue surge in the final quarter (September to December), driving massive volume compared to the initial months of the fiscal year.
